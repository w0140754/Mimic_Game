Mimic Prototype
Move with arrow keys or WASD. Chest: Space to exit, E to build. Shadow: stand on the chest tile to access your item bar, choose a held tile with the mouse or 1/2/3/4 (1 = empty), and press Space facing an empty adjacent tile to place it. Press F facing a tree to destroy it. Stand on the chest and press Space to re-enter it.
Tile: 0, 0
Build target: none
Use arrows/WASD to choose an adjacent empty tile. Press E or Esc to close.
